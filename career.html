Career:

At Nima saloon, we offer grade “A” haircuts and makeup. Our Slogan is “Grade A Quality For All” therefore we expect our team members to offer their best to our customers. Number one rule in our business is customer satisfaction, so our team members should treat customers with respect, integrity, and best performance. Working in Nima saloon is challenging , however it is rewarding too. Our employees are part of our family, so we do whatever it takes to make sure our employees improve their skills by attending different courses for free. Our team members usually have at least 5 years of experience however there are other positions that require lees experience. Employees usually after 5 years experience make up to 70K salary. Please take a look at our positions and apply por a position that your interested in using Email address in Contact Page.

Our Crew

Stylist Assistance: This position for stylist with less than 5 years’ experience. The best way to start gaining experience in this industry at a Pro level. General duties such as working on less sensitive areas of haircuts or makeups per stylist request, and helping maintain store cleanness .

Pro Stylist : This is a Professional position that requires minimum of 5 years’ experience. After 5 years working with us , you may be selected as store assistant manager based on your performance. You will work with customers for their haircut needs (including men and women ). You will be given at least one assistance to help you with less sensitive duties.

Makeup artist : Minimum of 5 years of makeup experience within professional workforce. You may be given paid interview if you are selected in Skype interview. After 5 years working with us , you may be selected as store assistant manager based on your performance. You will work with customers for their makeup needs (including men and women). You will be given at least one assistance to help you with less sensitive duties.
